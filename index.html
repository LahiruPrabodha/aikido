About Us

The Sri Lanka Aikido Association is committed to spreading the art and philosophy of Aikido, a Japanese martial art that fosters harmony, discipline, and personal growth. We offer a supportive and respectful environment for practitioners of all skill levels to explore and practice this unique martial art, with a focus on developing both physical abilities and inner resilience.
