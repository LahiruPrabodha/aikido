About Us

At the Sri Lanka Aikido Association, we are devoted to sharing the art of Aikido, a Japanese martial art that emphasizes harmony, discipline, and self-growth. Our association provides a nurturing and structured environment for practitioners of all levels, helping them develop both physical techniques and mental resilience through Aikido's principles.
